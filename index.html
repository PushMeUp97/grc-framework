What is GRC?

Governance, Risk & Compliance (GRC) is an integrated approach to managing organizational oversight, risk mitigation, and regulatory compliance.

🏛️

Governance

Establish clear governance structures, policies, and decision-making processes

⚠️

Risk

Identify, assess, and mitigate risks that could impact organizational objectives

✅

Compliance

Ensure adherence to applicable regulations and standards
