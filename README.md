# GRC Framework - Global Governance, Risk & Compliance Community

Welcome to the GRC Framework repository! This is an open-source, community-driven resource for understanding and implementing Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC) practices in modern working environments.

## 📚 What is GRC?

**Governance, Risk & Compliance (GRC)** is an integrated approach to managing organizational oversight, risk mitigation, and regulatory compliance. This framework helps organizations:

- Establish clear governance structures and policies
- Identify, assess, and mitigate risks
- Ensure compliance with applicable regulations and standards

## 🎯 Key Areas

### [SOC 2](./compliance/soc2/)
Security, Availability, Processing Integrity, Confidentiality, and Privacy Trust Services Criteria.

### [GDPR](./compliance/gdpr/)
General Data Protection Regulation requirements, Rights of Processing Agreements (ROPA), and Privacy-by-Design principles.

### [Policies](./compliance/policies/)
Corporate security policies including Access Control, Incident Response, and Data Protection.

### [Risk Management](./compliance/risk/)
Open risk management framework and practical templates for risk assessment and mitigation.
